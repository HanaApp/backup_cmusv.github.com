Welcome to Open Source SAP HANA App Community

This community is set up in collaboration with CMU Silicon Valley to explore the capabilities of SAP's in-memory HANA Database. Members can create, manage and share their HANA based applications on the platform of this community.

Please Note: Non members have only read access to the community applications. Please send email to cmusvhana@gmail.com for becoming a member of this community and creating a git repository (Project).
